Privacy Policy

What happens to the data you enter into this calculator — nothing leaves your device.

Last updated: March 2026

Short version: This site does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data you enter. All calculations run entirely in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.

Data Collection

This calculator has no backend server, no database, and no user accounts. Every number you enter — salary, commute details, tax information, financial obligations — is processed locally by JavaScript running in your browser. Your inputs are never sent to any server.

Cookies

This site does not set first-party cookies. Google AdSense (our advertising partner) may set third-party cookies for ad personalization. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings or by using the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool.

This site displays ads served by Google AdSense (publisher ID: ca-pub-5516736042033534). AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on browsing behavior. For more information, see Google's advertising policies and how Google uses data.

We comply with the IAB transparency and consent framework where applicable. EU/EEA users may see an additional consent prompt managed by Google's consent management platform.

Analytics

This site may collect aggregate, anonymous traffic data (page views, general geographic region) through standard server access logs or privacy-friendly analytics. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored in any analytics system.
