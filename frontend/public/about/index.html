About This Calculator

What Is My Time Worth? is a free, in-browser financial calculator that computes your real hourly wage — what you actually earn per hour of life energy spent on work, after accounting for taxes, hidden costs, and hidden time.

The calculator has three tools:

  • Real Hourly Wage — your actual effective wage after all hidden costs of employment
  • Is It Worth My Time? — a DIY vs. hire decision engine weighted by enjoyment
  • Compare Two Jobs — compare any two job offers on a true apples-to-apples basis

Methodology

The core methodology is inspired by Your Money or Your Life by Vicki Robin and Joe Dominguez. Their central insight: measure the cost of things in hours of life energy, not dollars. To do that, you need to know what an hour of your life is actually worth — not your advertised salary divided by 2,080, but your real take-home pay after every cost your job imposes, divided by every hour your job consumes.

Tax estimates use 2024/2025 IRS federal brackets with standard deductions and simplified flat-rate state tax approximations. FICA uses the employee share (7.65%). These are approximations — consult a CPA for your specific situation.

Privacy Policy

Short version: This site does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data you enter. All calculations run entirely in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.

Data collection

This calculator has no backend server, no database, and no user accounts. Every number you enter is processed locally by JavaScript running in your browser. Your salary, tax information, commute details, and other inputs are never sent to any server.
