Why This Calculator Exists

Most people have never calculated what an hour of their life is actually worth. Not their salary divided by 2,080 — their real take-home pay, minus every hidden cost of working, divided by every hour their job consumes. Commute time. Getting-ready time. Decompression time. Work lunches. Professional clothes. Once you know this number, every spending decision changes. A $40 dinner isn't $40 — it's X hours of your life energy.
